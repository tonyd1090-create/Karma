How to play / rules used
  • Each player gets 3 face-down cards, 3 face-up on top, and 3 in hand.
  • Swap phase: click a hand card then a face-up card to swap them. Put your weakest cards face up. Click Ready.
  • Play a card equal to or higher than the top of the pile. Stack multiple cards of the same rank at once.
  • While the deck lasts, draw back up to 3 after playing. Can't play? Pick up the whole pile.
  • Play your hand first, then face-up, then face-down (blind!).
  • ★ Magic: 2 resets the pile. 10 burns the pile and you go again. 7 forces the next player to play 7 or lower. Four of a rank in a row burns the pile.
  • First player to get rid of all cards wins — the game ends immediately.
